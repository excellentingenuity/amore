Packages & Services
We understand that there's no such thing as a "standard" event or a “standard” client.

That’s why we’ve designed packages with varying service levels, whether you want just a creative jump-start or full management of every last detail.

The Quick Start Package

Not sure where to begin planning for a wedding or other social event?
We can get you on the right track with just one meeting and a few crucial tools.

  • One face-to-face consultation (up to 2 hours)
  • Detailed budget worksheet
  • Customized timeline/ checklist
  • Vendor and site recommendations

The Big Day Package

This is perfect for the hands-on client who still wants to enjoy the special day once the planning is done. We’ll focus on making sure everything goes smoothly, so you can focus on making memories that last a lifetime.

  • One face-to-face consultation
  • Unlimited phone calls and e-mails the week of the event
  • Communication with all vendors to confirm timing and expectations
  • Full review of event setup to ensure that it meets specifications
  • Placement of seating cards, favors, pictures, sign-in book, programs, and other materials
  • Onsite coordinator to monitor the flow of the day

The All-Inclusive Package

If you don’t want to worry about a thing, this is the plan for you. From start to finish, we’re there each step of the way to make sure your vision becomes reality.

  • Up to 20 face-to-face consultations
  • Budget worksheet
  • Timeline and checklist
  • Vendor and venue recommendations
  • Communication with all vendors for timing and information
  • Day-of-event review to ensure the setup meets all specifications
  • Placement of seating cards, favors, pictures, sign in book, programs, and any other materials
  • Onsite coordinator to monitor the flow of the day

Individual Services

Let us help you on specific projects where you feel inexperienced or short of time. We offer expertise in all of the following areas, and more.

  • Vendor recommendations
  • Vendor coordination
  • Budget management
  • Day of event management
  • Site selection
  • Coordination of hotel room blocks
  • Floor plan creation
  • Guest gift bags
  • Favors
