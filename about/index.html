A head for numbers, A heart for people
That’s Amore.

Maria Amore
(864) 434-9006

Email Us

Following a career in finance, Maria got her first taste of the hospitality business as a front-desk clerk at a 114 room hotel. “How can I help you?” became ingrained in her vocabulary – and in her outlook.

With a relentless focus on caring customer service, Maria moved quickly up the ranks, taking on broader responsibilities at bigger properties. From Function Director to Catering Manager to Convention Services Manager, she gained an insider’s view on what it takes to create the perfect event.

Maria understands that every event is special, regardless of the budget or the head count. At the luxurious Westin Boston Waterfront Hotel, she managed international conferences with 3,000 attendees – but she brings the same level of caring to even the most intimate occasion.

Maria’s philosophy of caring extends to her personal life, as well. After losing her mother to cancer at a young age, she planned three personal fundraisers to raise thousands of dollars for the American Cancer Society.

If you’re looking for a planner who values results and relationships, contact Maria today.

You’ll appreciate the difference a little Amore  can make.
