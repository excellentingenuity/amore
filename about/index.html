Following a career in Finance, Maria got her first taste of the hospitality business as a front desk clerk at a 114 room hotel. With a relentless focus on caring customer service, Maria moved quickly up the ranks, taking on broader responsibilities at larger properties. From Function Director to Convention Services Manager, she has gained an insider's view on what it takes to create the perfect event.

Maria understands that every event is special, regardless of the budget or the head count. She managed domestic and international conferences that ranged from 2 attendees to 3,000 attendees. She brings the same level of caring and planning to any size occasion.

Maria's philosophy of caring extends to her personal life as well. After losing her mother to cancer at a young age, she planned three personal fundraisers and was able to send thousands of dollars to the American Cancer Society.

Maria has also been a member of the Association of Bridal Consultants since 2004.
